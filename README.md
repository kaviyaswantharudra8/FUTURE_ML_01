# FUTURE_ML_01: Sales & Demand Forecasting for Businesses

## 📌 Project Overview
This project builds a machine learning model to forecast future monthly sales and consumer demand using historical business transaction data. Designed to assist retail businesses in making data-driven operational decisions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Language & Tools:** Python, Jupyter Notebook
* **Libraries:** Pandas (Data cleaning), NumPy (Feature engineering), Scikit-learn (Machine Learning), Matplotlib (Visualization)
* **Algorithm:** Random Forest Regressor (Time-series lag feature approach)

## 📊 Business Insights & Use Cases
* **Inventory Management:** Prevents overstocking during projected low-revenue months and avoids stock shortages ahead of anticipated seasonal demand spikes.
* **Financial Planning:** Provides baseline monthly revenue projections to help allocate marketing budgets and operating expenses accurately.
* **Staffing Optimization:** Enables proactive scheduling of warehouse and retail staff based on forecasted high-volume shopping periods.

## 📈 Model Performance
* Evaluated using Mean Absolute Error (MAE) and Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) on a 20% test dataset split.
